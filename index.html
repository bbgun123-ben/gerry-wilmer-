Sketchbook Stories

Explore pages from my sketchbooks, each with its own story. These are the raw, unfiltered moments – drawn from life or memory – that often inspire my larger paintings. I hope you enjoy these little peeks into my creative journey!

Morning in the Rose Garden

Sketchbook, Pen & Watercolor. I woke up to the sound of birds and just had to capture my garden bathed in soft morning light. The dew on the rose petals and the chirping sparrows made this sketch a cherished memory of home.

Low Tide Stroll

Sketchbook, Pen & Wash. Sketched during a peaceful walk on the beach at low tide. The gentle hiss of the receding waves and the salty breeze are etched into this quick drawing. Whenever I look at it, I’m transported back to that quiet shore.

Forest Path in Rain

Sketchbook, Ink & Watercolor. A sudden summer rain found me sheltering under an oak. I used the raindrops in lieu of water for my paints! This sketch of the forest path, speckled by real raindrops, reminds me that nature often collaborates in my art.

Exhibition Timeline

  • 2025

    Herts Open Studios – St Albans

    Opened my home studio to the public as part of Herts Open Studios (Venue #29). Displayed original sketchbooks, giclée prints, and oil paintings; enjoyed meeting visitors and discussing my work.

  • 2019

    New Maynard Gallery Exhibition – Welwyn GC

    Group exhibition with local artists at New Maynard Gallery (28 Feb – 1 June 2019). Showcased a series of my paintings alongside works by Barbara Hanak and others. It was an honor to support a community gallery and even raise funds for charity during the show.

  • 2016

    Cambridge Open Art Exhibition – Cambridge

    Served as a judge for the Cambridge Open Art Exhibition. Reviewed and helped select the Top Twenty artworks of 2016. This experience gave me a new appreciation for the diverse talent in our art community.

  • 2015

    “Flora” Exhibit – Brookmans Park

    Participated in “Flora,” a group exhibition of paintings, illustrations, and photography focusing on botanical themes. My work was displayed among 10 Herts Visual Arts members at The Dutch Nursery (Feb 2–26, 2015) – one of my first public exhibition experiences.
